Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions regarding clinical sessions.

What should I expect during my first session?

Your initial consultation includes a comprehensive movement screening, orthopedic evaluation, strength testing, and static/dynamic postural analysis. Zaid will diagnose your issue, discuss goals, and begin initial manual treatment/corrective prescription on day one.

How long is each physical therapy visit?

Standard initial evaluations and sports treatment sessions run between 45 to 60 minutes. Specialized hands-on myofascial releases or follow-up wellness programs average 45 minutes of strict 1-on-1 therapist attention.

Do I need a doctor's referral to book?

In most cases, no. Under "Direct Access" regulations, you can receive physical therapy assessment and care directly. However, we will gladly coordinate with your primary care physician or orthopedic surgeon if needed.

Do you accept medical insurance?

We accept major PPO insurance plans and provide detailed "superbills" for patients claiming out-of-network reimbursement. Contact our clinic team directly, and we will verify your specific benefits details instantly.
